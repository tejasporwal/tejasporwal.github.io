Summary

The short version.

I am an engineer by training, a consultant by profession, and a builder by instinct.

I started by building AI systems at Samsung — shipping camera intelligence to millions of devices and working on patent-pending compiler optimizations for neural network inference. An MBA at Indian Institute of Management Bangalore (IIM Bangalore) and a strategy internship at Kearney India gave me a broader lens. Today, at Oliver Wyman India, I work on technology strategy for governments and financial institutions — problems where the stakes are real and the details matter.

In parallel, I build. Two ventures co-founded. One IEEE paper published. Experiments ongoing.
