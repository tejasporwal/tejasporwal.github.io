What management consultants get wrong about AI
Most of the frameworks I see in decks this year treat LLMs as a feature to be "integrated." They're closer to a new factor of production — and strategy people are going to keep mis-sizing the shift until they hold one long enough to feel it.
Published
—
Why I left engineering for strategy (and why I didn't really leave)
Most of the best strategists I know started as builders. Most of the best builders I know can run a decent 2×2. The gap between these tribes is more cultural than technical — and it's costing both.

The gap between AI demos and AI products
A demo is a function of the best ten inputs you can find. A product is a function of the worst thousand. Almost every AI failure I've seen in the last two years is that one sentence, repeated.
